KEEY - FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQ)

GETTING STARTED:

Q: How do I get started with Keey?
A: Simply call us at 0203 967 3687 or visit keey.co.uk/get-started to schedule a free consultation. We'll evaluate your property, provide an income estimate, and discuss how we can help.

Q: What types of properties do you manage?
A: We manage all types of residential properties: studios, 1-bedroom flats, 2-3 bedroom apartments, large houses, luxury properties, and investment portfolios. If it can be rented short-term, we can manage it.

Q: Do you only work with experienced Airbnb hosts?
A: No! We work with both experienced hosts and complete beginners. Whether you've never listed on Airbnb or you're a Superhost, we can help.

Q: What areas do you serve?
A: We operate in London (all zones) and Dubai, UAE. Contact us if you have properties in other locations.

Q: How long does it take to get my property listed?
A: Typically 7-10 days from signing up to going live. This includes photography, listing creation, and all setup procedures.

PRICING & FEES:

Q: How much does Keey charge?
A: Our management fee ranges from 12-20% of rental income depending on property size and type. This includes ALL services - no hidden fees. Studios/1-beds are typically 15-20%, while larger properties are 12-15%.

Q: Are there any setup fees?
A: No setup fees! The only one-time costs are professional photography (£200-400) and optional smart lock installation (£150-300).

Q: When do I pay your management fee?
A: We automatically deduct our fee from rental income before transferring the rest to you. You never pay us directly out of pocket.

Q: What if my property doesn't get bookings?
A: Our fee is percentage-based, so we only earn when you earn. We're highly incentivized to maximize your bookings. If you want guaranteed income regardless of bookings, ask about our Guaranteed Rent option.

Q: Are there cancellation fees?
A: No! We offer flexible month-to-month terms with 30 days notice. No lock-in contracts or penalty fees.

Q: Do you charge extra for cleaning or maintenance?
A: Regular cleaning after each guest is included. Minor maintenance is included. Major repairs (over £200) are handled at cost with your approval.

SERVICES & OPERATIONS:

Q: What exactly is included in your service?
A: Everything! Listing creation, photography, pricing optimization, 24/7 guest communication, vetting, check-ins, premium linens, cleaning, laundry, maintenance, review management, dedicated account manager, and more. Truly all-inclusive.

Q: Do you handle guest communication?
A: Yes, 24/7! We respond to all inquiries, bookings, questions, and issues. You never have to talk to guests unless you want to.

Q: What about cleaning?
A: Professional cleaning after every guest checkout is included. We also handle laundry, restocking supplies, and quality inspections.

Q: How do guests access the property?
A: Either through personal meet-and-greet check-ins (24/7 available) or via smart lock system. We recommend smart locks for maximum flexibility.

Q: What if something breaks?
A: We have a 24/7 on-call maintenance team. Emergency issues are handled within 2 hours. We coordinate all repairs and keep you informed.

Q: Do you provide linens and towels?
A: Yes! Hotel-quality linens, towels, and toiletries are included for every guest. Professionally laundered between stays.

Q: How do you screen guests?
A: We thoroughly vet each potential guest by reviewing their profile, past reviews, booking purpose, and red flags. We decline high-risk bookings to protect your property.

INCOME & PERFORMANCE:

Q: How much can I earn?
A: It depends on your property's location, size, and condition. Contact us for a free income estimate specific to your property. On average, owners earn 30-40% more with us than managing themselves.

Q: How is pricing determined?
A: We use dynamic pricing algorithms that adjust based on demand, seasonality, local events, competitor rates, and booking patterns. Our pricing analysts continually optimize for maximum revenue.

Q: How often will my property be booked?

Q: Can I block dates for personal use?
A: Absolutely! Your property, your rules. Just let us know when you want to use it, and we'll block those dates.

LEGAL & COMPLIANCE:

Q: Is short-term letting legal in London?
A: Yes, with certain regulations. In London, you can rent your primary residence for up to 90 days per year without planning permission. Investment properties may have different rules. We help ensure compliance.

Q: Do I need special insurance?
A: Yes, short-term rental insurance is recommended. We can advise on appropriate coverage. Our management includes guest liability coverage, but you should have proper property insurance.

Q: What about taxes?
A: You're responsible for reporting rental income to HMRC. We provide detailed financial statements to help with tax filing. We recommend consulting with a tax advisor familiar with rental properties.

Q: Do you handle regulatory compliance?
A: Yes, we stay updated on all local regulations and ensure your listing complies with current laws and platform policies.

Q: What if I have a mortgage on the property?

Q: Can you manage properties I already list on Airbnb?
A: Absolutely! We can take over existing listings and optimize them. Many clients come to us with existing Airbnb accounts that we enhance and manage.

Q: What if I'm overseas?
A: Perfect! Many of our clients are international investors. We handle everything remotely with full transparency through our owner portal.

Q: Do you offer property improvement advice?
A: Yes! We can recommend cost-effective improvements that increase rental income. Small upgrades often yield significant returns.

Q: What about furnished vs. unfurnished properties?
A: Properties must be furnished for short-term rental. If yours isn't, we can advise on furnishing packages that maximize ROI.
